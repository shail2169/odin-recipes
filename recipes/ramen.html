Ramen Recipe

A classic meal for any broke college student or something to eat in the middle of the night while waiting for another ranked game to queue up

Ingredients

  • Packet ramen from the grocery store (any flavor of choice)
  • Water
  • Pot
  • Optional: an egg

Steps

  1. In a pot pour in roughly 300-400ml of water and bring the water to a boil
  2. Once the water is boiling add the ramen flavouring packet and the ramen itself
  3. Cook for about 3-4 minutes or until you think the ramen is cooked
