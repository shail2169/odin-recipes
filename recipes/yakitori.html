Yakitori Recipe

Yakitori is a Japanese grilled chicken dish cooked on skewers and eaten with any type of sauce.

It is generally cooked on a grill with the chicken pieces cut into 1 inch cubes and coated in a sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 inch chicken cubes
  • 10 wooden skewers
  • Scallions
  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • Sugar
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Soak the 10 wooden skewers in cold water for 15 minutes
  2. Thread the cut 1 inch chicken pieces onto the skewers alternating with scallions
  3. Combine and mix the soy sauce, mirin, and sugar onto a pan and bringing it to a boil. Simmer for 5 minutes and reserve half for the sauce
  4. Heat a grill pan over high heat brushed with oil. Add skewers and cook until the chicken is cooked. Baste with half the sauce while cooking for 7 to 10 minutes
