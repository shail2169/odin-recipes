Mexican Strawberry Water Recipe

A Mexican drink with crushed strawberries or fruit to drink on a summer day.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of strawberries
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 8 cups of cold water
  • 5 cups of ice (or as needed)
  • 1 lime
  • 8 fresh mint sprigs

Steps

  1. Mix together strawberries, sugar, and water into a bowl and refrigerate for 4 hours
  2. After 4 hours, put the strawberry mix into a blender until smooth, and strain
  3. Fill glass with ice and pour drink into the class. Top off with lime, mint, and freshly cut strawberries
